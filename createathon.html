create-a-thon

a 48hr experiment in making & sharing

November 28-30, 2025



What is this?

Create-a-thon is a low-stakes weekend dedicated to trying things, making things, exploring ideas, and seeing where the process leads. It is open to anyone who feels the spark to create. No required experience. No specific medium. No pressure to finish or impress. This is not a competition: no judging, no rankings. Just creative practice, curiosity, and learning from each other.

You can make whatever you like: music, writing, video, code, games, sculpture, painting, performance, zines, culinary experiments, collage. You can stay in your comfort zone or use this as a chance to try something you have been curious about but never gave yourself permission to start.

You can work solo or form a small team and work together. Just as long as you create.


How it works

Sign up ahead of time. Space is limited to keep things intimate.

When you sign up, you will also submit a prompt or multiple prompt ideas. On Friday at 5pm, one prompt will be chosen at random and emailed to all participants. That is the creative spark for the weekend.

From Friday at 5pm to Sunday at 5pm, you make something. You do not have to use all 48 hours. Rest, do other life things, work at your own pace. There is no prize for pushing yourself too hard.

At the end, we gather in person for a show and tell afterparty.


Show and Tell Afterparty (Sunday, 5pm, Location TBD)

This is the heart of Create-a-thon. It is about the journey, not the destination.

Bring your work if possible, or bring documentation (photos, videos, audio, writing, screenshots) that helps give others a sense of what you made.

During your sharing time, you are also encouraged to speak to your creative process. These questions may help you reflect:

- How did you get started, and where did you go from there?

- What surprised you during the making?

- Where did things break or shift?

- What would you continue exploring if you had more time?

This is an opportunity to learn from each other across disciplines. A cross-pollination of creative thinking.


Rough Sunday Schedule

5:00–6:00 Gather and mingle

6:00–7:00 Show and tells

7:00–7:10 Short break

7:10–8:00 Remaining show and tells

8:00–9:00 Hang out, talk, unwind

A projector, mic, and a laptop will be available. The show and tells will be recorded for documentation and future reflection. You are welcome to invite friends, family, and fellow creators to attend.


Community During the Weekend

An optional Discord server will be open for those who want to share progress, frustrations, breakthroughs, or simple check-ins. No expectation to be active. It is simply there to build community before, during, and after the weekend.


Cost

Free. Truly. This is DIY. No budget. No entrance fee. Just people making things because that is what we are drawn to do.


What matters here

Process over product. Curiosity over perfection. Honesty over presentation. There is no failure when you create something. You learn, others learn through you, and that is the whole point.

If this resonates, you’re welcome here.
