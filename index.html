bio

jklynk, aka jonny klynkramer (he/they) is a toronto-based polydisciplanary artist and researcher whose practice drifts between interactive and generative media, installation, audio-visual performance, and DIY making.

comfortable moving between welding a bike-mounted projection system, composing soundscapes in ableton, or building real-time visuals in touchdesigner, they approach each project as an experiment: valuing process, curiosity, and the unexpected over polished outcomes.

shaped by nearly a decade living in the yukon and inspired more recently by toronto’s cycling culture and critical mass rides, jklynk’s recent work explores how technology, public space, and community intersect. this site is very much a work-in-progress: part portfolio, part open-source digital garden.
